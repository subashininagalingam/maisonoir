Our Story

A studio built on restraint.

Maison Noir opened in 2012 with a single chair and a belief that luxury in hair care is not about volume — it's about attention. Every stylist on our floor trains for a minimum of three years under a senior artist before taking solo clients. We keep our books deliberately small: fourteen stylists, one studio, no franchising.

Our palette — black, gold and ivory — reflects the same philosophy as our service: nothing loud, everything intentional.
